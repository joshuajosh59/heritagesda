HERITAGE SEVENTH-DAY ADVENTIST CHURCH

Heritage Seventh-Day Adventist Church Mile 3, is part of the Seventh-Day Adventist World Church. We are committed to showing the love of Jesus Christ and prepare people for His coming .

SPIRITUALITY

My God loves without restraint...

Many have tried to determine what constitutes the essential human endeavor. For some it is to be known and for others it is to be loved. For many it is to make a lasting mark on the world so that the impact of one's life continues to have significance.

STEPHEN CHAVEZ

Biker, Runner, and Pastor

One of the things I appreciate most about Jesus Christ was His ability to defy convention and live his own life, even though many considered it scandalous.

I haven’t tried to be scandalous, but I have tried to defy convention about what it means to be a pastor, and by extension, a Christian. For me, Christians aren’t just people who ascribe to a system of beliefs -- they embrace life and accept its many challenges.
